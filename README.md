Privacy Policy
Effective Date: April 19, 2026
This Privacy Policy explains how our Discord application (the "Bot") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our services.
1. Information We Collect
We only collect the minimum amount of data necessary for the Bot to function. This may include:
User Information: User IDs, usernames, and avatars.
Server Information: Server IDs and channel IDs.
Message Content: Only processed in real-time for commands; we do not store message history permanently unless specifically required for a feature (like logging).
2. How We Use Information
We use the collected data to:
Execute commands requested by the user.
Maintain server-specific settings and configurations.
Ensure the security and stability of the Bot.
3. Data Sharing and Disclosure
We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. Data is only shared:
To comply with Discord's official legal requirements.
If required by law or a valid legal process.
4. Data Retention and Deletion
We only store data for as long as necessary to provide our services.
User Rights: You can request the deletion of your data at any time by leaving the support server or contacting the developer directly.
If the Bot is removed from a server, all data associated with that server is typically deleted within 30 days.
5. Third-Party Services
Our Bot operates on the Discord platform. By using this Bot, you are also subject to the Discord Privacy Policy.
6. Contact Us
If you have any questions regarding your data or wish to request data deletion, please contact the developer via the support server or the contact info on the Bot's profile.
